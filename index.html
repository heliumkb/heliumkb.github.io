GOALS

Better than make things up on the go
There's so many great and awesome options that could fuel an entire new keycap set, but here's those that filter out irrelevant ones.

  • PBT is a must.
  • DSA because with many odd layouts that sculptured doesn’t make sense. More love to unique board, right?
  • New set of pictograms for consistency in all pictogram modifier keys.
  • Use of good font - DIN compliant typeface. Font and typography are important and was often treated with low priority in other sets. Nice proportion between different legend types and pictograms and the keycaps itself.
  • Nice colorway that subtle enough to use daily, but difference enough to start some conversations.
  • Cover most of normal usages (and desired aesthetic for layouts) without using too many child sets.
  • Avoid blank - legend mix at all cost. Every time I looked at Ergodox set with blank modifiers; I feel patchy, incomplete, unsatisfying stuff that drive me to work on this one.
  • Avoid use of wrong legends for user if possible.
  • Retro looking is a plus.

PROCESS

The Colorway
Many colors, only some will do.

I exlore combination from all tones, but there's very few nice mute/toned down color After a series of trial for a pair of light/dark I settled with these two primary colors: TGJ and TT. Retro, easy on eyes, light enough for readable sublimated legends., ce the name ‘Pumpkin Spice’
